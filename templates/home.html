What is Social Butterfly?

Social Butterfly allows you to anonymously chat with random strangers through Google Talk. If you use Gmail, you already have Google Talk.

How do I sign up?

You’ve successfully signed up.

Social Butterfly has sent you a chat invitation. Please accept the invitation, then Social Butterfly will add itself to your buddy list.

What’s next?

Once Social Butterfly is on your buddy list, send it a message with only the text “/help” (without the quotes). Chat with strangers!
